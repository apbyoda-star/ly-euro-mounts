The Mount

What Is a European Mount?

A European mount is a clean, natural way to display your trophy by preserving only the skull and antlers or horns. Unlike traditional taxidermy mounts, there is no hide, cape, or artificial features — just the real bone structure of the animal, professionally cleaned and finished for display.

This process involves carefully removing all tissue, fully degreasing the skull, and whitening the bone to achieve a bright, uniform appearance without damaging its integrity. The end result is a sharp, long-lasting mount that highlights the true character of the animal.

European mounts have become increasingly popular for their simplicity, durability, and modern look. They offer a more affordable option than shoulder mounts while still providing a high-quality display that fits well in homes, shops, and lodges.

Whether mounted on a plaque or displayed in a more natural setting, a European mount is a timeless way to preserve the memory of your hunt.
